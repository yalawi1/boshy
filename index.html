About

“Forever grateful for the journey — and proud to be a representative of what modesty means.”

Boshra is a modest fashion model working with brands across the region, bringing warmth, grace and a distinctly editorial eye to every shoot. Alongside modeling, she practices interior design — curating calm, considered spaces in the same soft, timeless palette she wears.

Interiors

Calm, considered spaces — styled with the same softness

Interior design is Boshra's second language — neutral palettes, natural textures and light that moves through a room like fabric. From single-room refreshes to full concepts, every space is styled to feel effortless.
